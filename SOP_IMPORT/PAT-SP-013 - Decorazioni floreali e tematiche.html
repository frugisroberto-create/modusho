1. Floral compositions throughout palazzo follow strict brand guidelines for consistency and aesthetic.
  2. Composition sizing: contained and proportionate, never excessive or overwhelming.
  3. Color palette: refined, never overly colorful; harmonious with season and palazzo aesthetic.
  4. Floral preference: local and Mediterranean flowers (not commercial tropical species).
  5. Include branches, foliage, and green elements; compositions appear spontaneous, organic (never constructed/artificial).
  6. Flowers must never obstruct sightlines, views, or guest pathways.
  7. Fragrance level: subtle and pleasant; never invasive, overpowering, or artificial-smelling.
  8. Flowers must never demand visual attention or compete with architectural features.
  9. Refreshment schedule: lobby, library, restaurant minimum every 3 days; guest rooms at each new arrival.
  10. Seasonal themes aligned with palazzo calendar and natural seasons (not commercial holidays).
  11. Supplier relationship: cultivate partnership with local florist(s) for consistency and sustainability.
  12. Budget managed monthly with Director oversight.
  13. Staff trained on floral placement principles and seasonal update procedures.
  14. Brand message: "I fiori danno vita allo spazio senza rubargli la scena." (Flowers bring life to space without stealing its spotlight.)
