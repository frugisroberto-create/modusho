1. Welcome amenities differentiated by room category and guest type.
  2. Standard rooms: Patria Palace welcome card, bottled water, seasonal fresh fruit.
  3. Superior/Deluxe rooms: Standard items plus local artisanal product (olive oil, taralli, artisanal chocolate, etc.).
  4. Suite rooms: All previous items plus enhanced selection (wine or prosecco, floral arrangement, handwritten note from Hotel Manager).
  5. Repeat guests: personalized touch based on guest profile and history (favorite beverage, preferred snack, book recommendation, etc.).
  6. VIP guests and LHW leader members: bespoke amenity coordinated with Guest Relations and Direzione.
  7. All amenities presented on branded tray or elegant surface consistent with palazzo aesthetic.
  8. Welcome note personalized with guest name; handwritten notes prepared where possible (especially suites and VIP).
  9. Amenities placed in room before guest arrival and verified for quality and freshness.
  10. Housekeeping confirms placement and reports back to Guest Relations.
  11. Brand message: "L'amenity non è un omaggio. È un gesto di riconoscimento." (Amenity is not a gift. It is a gesture of recognition.)
