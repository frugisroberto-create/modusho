1. First-time guests invited to library upon arrival or shortly after check-in for welcome moment.
  2. Select return guests also invited based on profile and Guest Relations judgment.
  3. Guest Relations or Front Office Manager greets guest personally and warmly.
  4. Brief palazzo orientation delivered through natural conversation (not formal tour or recitation).
  5. Offer beverage selection: tea, coffee, welcome cocktail, or refreshment of guest preference.
  6. Introduce key services and experiences subtly and naturally: spa, restaurant, signature experiences, cultural offerings.
  7. Provide personalized local recommendations based on guest interests: exhibitions, artisans, restaurants, cultural sites.
  8. Moment kept natural and unhurried; adapt to guest energy and availability.
  9. If guest is tired from travel, keep welcome moment brief and offer to continue later.
  10. This is a relationship-building moment, not a sales pitch; focus on connection and understanding.
  11. Personalized welcome amenity offered if applicable (based on occasion or guest profile).
  12. Brand message: "La library è il primo spazio intimo del palazzo. Il welcome moment che vi si svolge è il primo atto dell'ospitalità Patria." (Library is first intimate space. Welcome moment is first act of Patria hospitality.)
